01

Brand Vision

GOEYE should not be viewed as just another eyewear brand. My goal is for it to become an elevated lifestyle movement with genuine cultural impact — a name people integrate seamlessly into their daily lives, one that sets trends on major runways, is worn by top models, and carries prestige that extends well beyond the fashion industry.

We are not simply selling glasses; we are building an iconic identity.

To scale this vision, our Instagram strategy needs a precise, high-energy balance between premium aesthetics and indirect, narrative-driven marketing.

02

Instagram Audit Findings

What's Working

01

A Wide, Clearly Showcased Product Portfolio

GOEYE's Instagram effectively showcases a diverse range of eyewear, including prescription glasses, sunglasses, and sports and performance eyewear.

02

A Strong Myth-Busting Content Angle

Messaging such as “Carrots fix your vision. Dim light ruins your eyes. Glasses make you dependent — most of what you've heard is wrong” is confident and scroll-stopping.

03

A Clear, Ownable Bio Positioning

GOEYE's bio doesn't read like every other eyewear seller — it stakes a claim on being the brand that tells people “what nobody else tells you”.

Areas Of Improvement

Build a Strong Brand Community

The page currently functions primarily as a content and product showcase. To drive long-term engagement and customer loyalty, GOEYE should invest in recurring content series, customer spotlights, interactive Stories, polls, challenges, and a branded hashtag.

Optimize Profile & Content Fundamentals

The profile can be strengthened with a clearer value proposition and stronger call-to-action in the bio.

Shift from Direct Selling to Storytelling

Instagram rewards content that entertains before it sells. GOEYE should build storytelling-led content where eyewear becomes a natural part of the story.

MYTH vs REALITY: Blue Lenses Edition

A fast-cut, card-flip format.

MYTH: “Blue light lenses block 100% of screen damage.”
REALITY: they cut glare and ease end-of-day eye fatigue — they're not a forcefield.

MYTH: “Any yellow-tinted lens does the same thing.”
REALITY: a true blue-cut coating works nothing like a cosmetic tint.

Each MYTH card snaps into its REALITY card with a satisfying on-screen flip.

CTA: “Comment 'BLUE' if you believed Myth #1 too 👀”
